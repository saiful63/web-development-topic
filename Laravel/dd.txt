dd stands for "Dump and Die."

Laravel's dd() function can be defined as a helper function, which is used to dump a variable's contents to the browser and prevent the further script execution.

Example:

dd($array); 


What Is a Route? The route is a way of creating a request URL for your application. These URLs do not have to map to specific files on a website, and are both human readable and SEO friendly. In Laravel, routes are created inside the routes folder. They are are created in the web.
